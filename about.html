About US

Our Vision: A Hunger-Free Society and Brighter Future for Orphans

At Love and Service @ Your Aid, we dream of creating a world where hunger is no longer a barrier to a better life and where every orphan can thrive with dignity. We believe that every child, especially orphans, deserves a chance at a better way of living, and we're committed to taking tangible steps toward making this dream a reality.

Our Mission: Providing Essentials for Orphans

Having observed the difficult conditions in orphanages—where basic necessities such as clothing, bedding, fans, books, and other essentials are often in short supply—we decided to act. Our organization partners directly with orphanages, raising funds to supply these critical needs on a project-by-project basis. By doing so, we aim to create an environment where children can focus on growing and learning, free from the challenges of insufficient resources.

Why We Exist: Bridging the Gap Between NGOs and the Needy

While many NGOs and charities are working tirelessly to serve those in need, we have noticed that in many cases, their resources don't always reach their intended targets. That's where we come in. Love and Service @ Your Aid acts as a bridge, ensuring that assistance gets to the children who need it most. We collaborate with NGOs to extend their reach, ensuring no child is left behind.

Our Belief: Small Contributions, Big Impact

We strongly believe in the power of collective action. As we often say: "Can anyone believe that we can make dollars for a rupee?" "Yes… By using a rupee wisely, we can bring a million-dollar smile to a child's face." This idea is central to everything we do. Small contributions, when used effectively, can make an extraordinary difference in the lives of orphans, and together, we can achieve more than we ever imagined.

Join Us: Together, We Can

At Love and Service @ Your Aid, we believe that the collective efforts of people with a shared vision can change the world. We invite you to join us on this journey to create a brighter, hunger-free future for orphans. Yes, together we can…
